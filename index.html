Advocate John Mwangi, LLB, LLM

With over 15 years of practice in Kenyan courts, Advocate John Mwangi leads Nairobi Legal Chambers with a commitment to justice and client-focused representation.

Admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2008, he specializes in civil litigation, corporate law, and constitutional matters, having represented clients in landmark cases before the High Court and Court of Appeal.
